What is GINA?

Get INdigenous trees to Adopt


According to the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, the Phlippines loses approximately 47,000 hectares of forest cover yearly. GINA revolves around addressing the country's need for tree planters with its key features.

GINA gives awareness to the users of the problem and allows its users to assist in solving it. The application enables the users to access to a variety of tree species and seeds and provides the users the opportunity to plant and share details about their planted seeds.

When using GINA, points can be earned for every seed purchase and every yearly documentation. These points give users incentives in the future.
